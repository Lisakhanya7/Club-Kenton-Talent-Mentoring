About Club Kenton Talent Mentoring

Located in the heart of Ekuphumleni, Kenton-On-Sea, Eastern Cape

Our Mission

To develop young talent through quality football training while providing comprehensive mentoring to help children excel academically, socially, and personally. We believe in building champions who make a positive impact in their communities.

Our Vision

To create a nurturing environment where every child from Ekuphumleni and Kenton-On-Sea can unlock their potential, pursue their dreams, and become role models in their families and communities.

Our Programs

Comprehensive Development for Young Talent

Elite Football Academy

Professional coaching with international standards. Ages 6-18. Training focuses on technical skills, tactical awareness, and fitness development. Regular matches and tournaments throughout the season.

  • Expert Coaching
  • Modern Facilities
  • Competitive Matches

Academic Support

Dedicated tutoring programs in Mathematics, English, and Sciences. Free after-school study sessions. Mentorship from high-achieving professionals. Goal: Improve grades and boost confidence.

  • One-on-One Tutoring
  • Study Groups
  • Homework Support

Life Skills & Mentoring

Character development workshops. Leadership training. Career guidance and counseling. Healthy lifestyle seminars. Building confidence and resilience for a better future.

  • Career Guidance
  • Leadership Programs
  • Wellness Sessions

Youth Development

Holistic development combining sports and education. Friendship building and team unity. Community service initiatives. Building responsible citizens for tomorrow.

  • Team Building
  • Community Service
  • Social Events
