About Club Kenton Talent Mentoring

Located in the heart of Ekuphumleni, Kenton-On-Sea, Eastern Cape

What CLUB KTM Is

A community club that combines sports, education, and wellness.

The idea is to use sports not just for fun, but as a way to teach life skills, build confidence, and guide young people toward success.

It started in January 2014 in Kenton-on-Sea.

Our Programs & Teams

Wide Range of Sports and Development Programs

Soccer Academy

Our flagship program with six competitive teams covering all age groups: U11, U13, U15, U17 Boys, Senior Men, and U13 Girls. Daily afternoon training sessions developing technical skills, tactical awareness, and team spirit.

  • Boys U11, U13, U15, U17
  • Girls U13 & Senior Teams
  • Professional Coaching

Education & Academic Support

Comprehensive homework support classes and tutoring programs. We help young athletes balance sports excellence with academic achievement, ensuring they succeed in the classroom as well as on the field.

  • Homework Support Classes
  • After-School Tutoring
  • Academic Mentorship

Tennis Program

Open to young athletes aged 8-18. Beginner practice sessions on Monday and Thursday afternoons, with competitive matches on Friday. Professional coaching develops technique, speed, and mental toughness.

  • Ages 8-18
  • Beginners to Advanced
  • Weekly Matches

Netball Program

Separate divisions for Juniors (U10-U17) and Seniors. Multiple practice sessions throughout the week develop ball control, positioning, and team coordination. Competitive tournaments and friendly matches.

  • Junior Divisions U10-U17
  • Senior Teams
  • Weekly Competition

Rugby Program

Teams for U13 and U15 age groups with afternoon practice sessions. Building strength, courage, and camaraderie through this dynamic sport. Professional coaching ensures safe and skillful play.

  • U13 Team
  • U15 Team
  • Skill Development

Cricket & Other Sports

Mini cricket program for young players with afternoon practice sessions. We also offer wellness programs including fitness, aerobics, and environmental initiatives like ocean clean-ups and gardening projects.

  • Mini Cricket for Kids
  • Wellness & Fitness
  • Environmental Programs

Life Skills & Mentoring

Character development workshops, leadership training, and career guidance. We help young people build confidence, resilience, and critical thinking skills for life beyond sports.

  • Leadership Training
  • Career Guidance
  • Life Coaching

Community & Youth Development

Community tournaments, special events, and service initiatives. Our programs build friendship, team unity, and social responsibility, creating positive young citizens committed to their communities.

  • Community Tournaments
  • Social Events
  • Community Service

  • Expand Outreach Programs - Reach more youth in the community
