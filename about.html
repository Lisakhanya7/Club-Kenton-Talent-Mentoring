Our Mission

To educate, train, coach, capacitate and encourage members of the club and community through sport so that all members have the maximum opportunity to realise their full potentials.

Our Vision

To be recognised locally, regionally, nationally & internationally for producing guided, educated talent who are capacitated to be future leaders.

History

Established in January 2014 in Kenton-on-Sea, Club KTM is dedicated to holistic youth development through sports and education.
