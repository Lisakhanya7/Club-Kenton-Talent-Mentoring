About Club Kenton Talent Mentoring

Located in the heart of Ekuphumleni, Kenton-On-Sea, Eastern Cape

What Club KTM Is

Club Kenton Talent Mentoring (KTM) is a vibrant community organization in Kenton-On-Sea, Ekuphumleni, Eastern Cape, that combines sports and education to transform young lives. We use sports as a powerful tool to help young people grow, stay active, and learn essential life skills. Our holistic approach focuses on developing athletic talent while preparing the next generation to become confident, educated leaders who can make a positive impact in their communities.

Our Mission

"To educate, develop, train, coach, capacitate and encourage members of the club and community through sport and educational programs so that all members have the maximum opportunity to realise their full potentials."

Our Vision

To be recognized locally and internationally for producing talented, educated young leaders. We envision transforming the lives of young people by nurturing their talents in sports and academics, preventing them from falling into crime or drug abuse, and giving them opportunities to become future leaders and role models.

Our Core Values

  • Excellence in Development: Commitment to developing every young person to their full potential
  • Transparency & Accountability: Operating with integrity in all our activities
  • Community Focus: Creating a positive impact on the local community
  • Holistic Growth: Balancing sports, education, and life skills development
